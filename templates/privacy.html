Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. Data We Collect

To provide Spendly, we collect the account details you give us when you register — such as your name and email address — and the expense information you choose to record, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We only collect what is needed to operate the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to deliver the Service: to authenticate your account, store your expenses, and present your spending back to you through summaries and breakdowns. We do not sell your personal data or use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your information is stored securely and retained only for as long as your account remains active. We take reasonable measures to protect it against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. You may request deletion of your data at any time, after which it will be removed from our active systems.

4. Third Party Services

We may rely on trusted third-party providers to host and operate Spendly. These providers process data only on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes. We do not share your personal financial data with third parties except as needed to run the Service or as required by law.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app and we will be happy to help.
